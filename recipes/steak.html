Pan Seared Steak With Garlic and Rosemary Garnish

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Steak (Ribeye or Newyork strip
  • 1/2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Sprig Rosemary

How to Pan Sear Steaks

  1. Pat Dry â€“use paper towels to pat the steaks dry to get a perfect sear and reduce oil splatter
  2. Preheat the pan-on medium and brush with oil
  3. Sear Steaks-add steaks and sear each side 3-4 minutes until a brown crust has formed
  4. Add butter and aromatics -smelt in butter with quartered garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs. Tilt pan to spoon garlic butter over steaks and cook to your desired doneness
  5. Remove steak and rest 10 minutes before slicing against the grain.

Pro Tip: We prefer using a large cast-iron pan for best heat conduction, but a large heavy stainless steel pan will also work.
